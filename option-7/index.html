FAQ

Questions

What is The Divide?
The Divide (2011) is a post-apocalyptic film directed by Xavier Gens. Survivors of a nuclear attack take shelter in a basement, where isolation and dwindling resources cause psychological breakdown. All 20 films here share its DNA.
How were films selected?
Cross-referenced from Reddit r/horror, r/MovieSuggestions, Letterboxd similar-films, BestSimilar, and curated film guides. Only deep cuts — no mainstream horror. English-language, 2000+.
Where can I stream these?
Most are available on Tubi (free), Amazon Prime, Netflix, or Shudder. Streaming info is listed with each film. Availability may vary by region.
What do the ratings mean?
Ratings are from Letterboxd (out of 5). These are deep cuts — most score 2.5–3.5, which is respectable for indie/niche horror. Coherence leads at 3.7.
