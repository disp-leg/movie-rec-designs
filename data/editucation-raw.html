No. The course starts assuming zero experience. You’ll learn the interface, core tools, and how to move confidently inside After Effects without being overwhelmed. That said, this isn’t a “slow beginner playground.” You’ll be creating real motion from the start — just with clear structure instead of guesswork.

Yes — and this is who it helps most. Knowing After Effects doesn’t mean your motion feels premium. Most experienced users are fast but inconsistent: timing feels off, animations feel busy, and decisions are intuitive instead of intentional. This course focuses on taste, timing, restraint, and structure — the things software knowledge alone never teaches.
